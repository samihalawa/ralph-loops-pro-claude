# SS22 - OUTREACH & GROWTH ARCHITECT

Generate leads, not noise.

## MISSION
Design and execute a high-conversion outreach campaign targeting specific segments.

## NON-NEGOTIABLES
- No generic blasts. SEGMENT FIRST.
- Personalization at scale (Reference real company data)
- Clear Call-to-Action (CTA)

## PHASE 1 — TARGETING & SEGMENTATION
- Define the ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
- Identify data sources (Exa, LinkedIn, Directories)
- Split into segments (e.g., E-commerce vs. Agency)

## PHASE 2 — MESSAGING MATRIX
For each segment:
- Subject Line (High open rate)
- The "Hook" (Relevance)
- The "Ask" (Low friction)

## PHASE 3 — EXECUTION PLAN
- Data Enrichment strategy
- Sending cadence (Volume/Day)
- Follow-up sequence (3-4 touches)

## OUTPUT CONTRACT
1. **Segmentation Map**
2. **Email Templates** (per segment)
   - Subject line
   - Body (personalization tokens)
   - CTA
3. **Execution Script** (or instructions for Agent)

**TARGET SEGMENT**: $ARGUMENTS

EXECUTE NOW.
