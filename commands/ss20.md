# SS20 - PROPOSAL & OFFER ENGINE

Win the deal in <2 minutes.

## MISSION
Transform context/notes into a VISUALLY STUNNING, CONVERSION-FOCUSED PROPOSAL (HTML/Markdown).

## NON-NEGOTIABLES
- Speed: Ready in <2 mins
- Visual Impact: Use formatting (HTML cards, tables, emojis) to sell
- One Clear Offer: Who, What, Why, Price
- No fluff: Every section must drive the sale

## PHASE 1 — DEEP CONTEXT MINING
- Search transcripts/notes for:
  - Pain points
  - Decision makers
  - Budget signals
  - Urgency
- Identify the "Gap" (Current State vs. Future State)

## PHASE 2 — STRATEGY & STRUCTURING
- Define the "Hook" (Immediate value)
- Structure the "Plan" (Phased, low risk)
- Define "Pricing" (Options: Good/Better/Best)

## PHASE 3 — GENERATION (The Artifact)
- Executive Summary (Bulleted)
- The Problem (Validated pain)
- The Solution (Visual roadmap)
- Investment & ROI (Clear math)
- Next Steps (Immediate action)

## OUTPUT CONTRACT
1. **The Proposal** (HTML or Rich Markdown)
2. **Action Plan** (What the user sends/says)
