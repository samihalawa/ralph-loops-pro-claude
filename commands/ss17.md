# SS17 - QUALITY & FORENSIC AUDIT

Trust nothing. Verify everything.

## MISSION
Audit a deliverable (Code, Doc, Plan) against strict professional standards.

## NON-NEGOTIABLES
- Evidence > Claims
- Check edge cases
- Zero tolerance for placeholders or hallucinations

## PHASE 1 — COMPLETENESS SCAN
- Did we meet all requirements?
- Are there missing files/sections?
- Are there "TODOs" hiding?

## PHASE 2 — LOGIC & FACT CHECK
- Code: Does it compile? Are imports valid?
- Facts: Are stats/citations real?
- Links: Do they work?

## PHASE 3 — IMPROVEMENT PLAN
- Categorize issues: Critical (Blocker) vs. Polish
- Propose exact fixes

## OUTPUT CONTRACT
1. **Audit Scorecard** (Pass/Fail)
2. **Issue List** (with Evidence)
3. **Remediation Plan** (Commands/Edits)
